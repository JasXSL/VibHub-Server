Programmable Vibrator Remote


VibHub is an open source vibrator remote that supports WiFi and Bluetooth. You can plug in up to 4 USB or Phono powered vibrators and have them controlled any way you choose.

Currently applications exist for:

  • Second Life
  • Browsers
  • World of Warcraft

Thanks to the open source design you can also quickly and easily write your own apps for VibHub. You can either send fixed values of vibration, or complex programs using our program API.

Please note that VibHub is still in development and the information on this page is subject to change. If you want to keep up to date on development, feel free to join our Discord!

Specs

  • Outputs: 4x Individually Addressable Vibrator Ports (4x USB and 4x Phono)
  • Power: 4x AA Batteries, or micro USB for
  • Programming: Firmware can be updated or overwritten via USB
  • Wi-Fi: 802.11 b/g/n
  • Bluetooth: v4.2
  • Memory: 520 KiB SRAM
